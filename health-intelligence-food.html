Food Intelligence

brief synopsis about what this covers

Evidence-based food recommendations

Below is a summary of our individual recommendations for your diet, based on your analysis results, the health interview and the current scientific research. These recommendations are customized for you and are divided into 7 categories to make it easier for you to implement in your daily diet plan. For each category, we also provide our general dietary recommendations, including the essential foods that we believe everyone should include in their diet, as well as foods that we recommend avoiding.

Fruit and Vegetables
 
What you need to Eat

Oranges, Grapefruit, Strawberry, Tomato, Red pepper, Broccoli, Spinach, Peas, Brussels sprout, Brightly colored fruits and vegetables, Cabbage, Asparagus, Mushrooms, Soybeans, Lenses, Carrots, Avocado, aspberry, Sweet potato, Cauliflower, Rocula, Salad, Broccoli sprouts, Green beans, Lima beans, Legumes, Dark leafyvegetables.

Essential Foods

: Broccoli, Arugula, Spinach, Mushrooms, Avocado, Olives, Blueberries, Broccoli sprouts, Sprouts and Herbs

What you should avoid

Potatoes, Fruit juice, Fruit with a high sugar content and Corn

Meat and Dairy products
 
What you need to Eat

Organ meat, beef, Lamb, Veal, Turkey, Chicken, Milk, Cheese, Beef Liver, Egg, Liver, Red Meat.

Essential Foods

Eggs, Meat and Poultry

Meat that is not organic or free-range, Processed meat, Cold cuts, Sausages, Bacon and Corn fed beef
